Key takeaways:

1. **iOS-emit side restriction holds** — iPhone logs successful
   `iOS_HYBRID_STARTED + WINDOW_COMPLETE` while neither carrier
   (manufacturer-data MB cue with company `0xFFFF`, nor `…1001`
   service data) reaches the receiver's decode layer on either
   Android device. Matches the
   `docs/ble_carrier_decision.md` finding about CoreBluetooth
   foreground restrictions on third-party apps.
2. **T390 receive-path question** — T390 also missed R52's MB cues
   in recapture-5b (where iPhone wasn't involved). That's a separate
   T390-specific receive triage item — possibly Android-9 ScanRecord
   parsing, possibly bench RF positioning. Independent of the
   carrier decision.
3. **The shim is solid** — three consecutive paired runs reached
   the test body and exercised the production scanner end-to-end.
   API-28 instrumented-test coverage is unblocked from this session
   forward.

The carrier-decision conclusion stays as locked in commit `2d9cf11`:
**MB legacy beacon + GATT fetch is the only validated production
iOS↔Android route.** Direct-MX hybrid carrier remains `:rejected`
